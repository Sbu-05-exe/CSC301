Top 5 Restaurants

  1. The Rat and Parrot
    2. A great place to eat out with friends and celebrate finishing big tests or assignments. Awesome food, and great atmosphere.
  2. Gino's
    3. Best place to get pizza in town. Just be wary of long wait times during peak hours.
  3. Fork And Dagger
    4. If you want to impress that special person by breaking the bank. This is the place to go.
  4. Major Fraser's
    5. A chilled place to hang with friends and grab a coffee. A popular place to eat among families. A nice place to have a farewell meal that marks your leaving the nest.
  5. The Red Cafe
    6. A great place to grab a quick lunch between lectures and tutorials or practicals.

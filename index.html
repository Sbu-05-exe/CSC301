Welcome to Makhanda!

From newly-arrived students to well-seasoned travellers, Makhanda offers many historical attractions and fine dining for people of all colours places and different shoes laces. This small town has an aura that is difficult not to get attached to. From the eccentric weather to the amiable residents of the area, you are destined to embark on a spirited advetures that will leave you with memories that will last a lifetime!
